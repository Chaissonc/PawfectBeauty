Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Pawfect Beauty, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the message you provide. We do not collect payment information — all bookings are handled through our third-party booking platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. Messages submitted through our form are subject to Formspree's privacy policy. Our website also embeds a Google Maps iframe on our contact and about pages. Your use of the map is subject to Google's privacy policy. Appointments are booked through Groomer.io, which has its own privacy policy.

Cookies

Pawfect Beauty does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools on this website. Third-party embeds (Google Maps, Groomer.io) may set their own cookies when you interact with them.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. We do not maintain a database of visitor information.
