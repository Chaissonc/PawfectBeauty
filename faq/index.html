Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

The easiest way is to book online through our booking page — just click "Book Online" in the nav. You can also call us at (702) 954-6226 or email us at Pawfectbeautylv@gmail.com and we'll get you scheduled.

Do I need to bring vaccine records?

Yes — all vaccine records are required at drop off. Please make sure your dog is current on vaccinations before your appointment. If you're unsure what's needed, feel free to give us a call ahead of time.

How long does a grooming appointment take?

It depends on the service and your dog's size and coat. A Bath & Nails is typically 1–1.5 hours. A Bath & Tidy runs about 1.5–2 hours. A Full Groom usually takes 2–3 hours. If you add Express Service, your dog is groomed straight through with no kennel wait time, so the process is often faster.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes! We welcome walk-ins when our schedule allows. We always recommend booking ahead to guarantee your spot, but if you're nearby and want to stop in, we'll do our best to accommodate you. Walk-in availability can vary by day, so calling ahead is always a good idea.

My dog is anxious or nervous — can you still help?

Absolutely. Jenny and Mihsha have years of experience working with nervous, anxious, senior, and rescue dogs — including dogs who've been turned away elsewhere. We work at your dog's pace and make sure they feel safe from the moment they walk in. Just let us know about your dog's needs when you book and we'll plan accordingly. We also offer Express Service, which keeps your dog out of a kennel entirely, which many anxious pups find much more comfortable.

What's included in a Full Groom?

Our Full Groom includes a soothing bath, a custom haircut (breed standard or your preference), ear cleaning, nail trim and dremel, tidy paws and sanitary trim, and anal gland expression. Basically everything your pup needs to look and feel their best.

How often should I groom my dog?

It depends on your dog's breed and coat type. For long-haired or double-coated breeds like Doodles, Poodles, and Shih Tzus, we generally recommend a Full Groom every 6–8 weeks to prevent matting. Short-coated breeds can usually go longer between grooms but still benefit from a regular Bath & Tidy. When in doubt, ask us at your appointment and we'll recommend a schedule for your specific dog.

Do you groom all breeds and sizes?

Yes — we groom dogs of all breeds and sizes, from tiny Chihuahuas to large breeds. Our pricing is based on size (X-Small through Extra Large), and we tailor every groom to your dog's individual coat, temperament, and needs.

What is Express Service?

Express Service is a $20 add-on to any appointment. Your dog is groomed start to finish by one groomer without being placed in a kennel between steps. It's especially popular for anxious dogs, senior pups, and dogs who've had rough experiences at other salons.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and all major credit cards.
