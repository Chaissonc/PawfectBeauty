We Pamper Your Pet to Pawfection

Pawfect Beauty is a women-owned dog grooming salon in Las Vegas, NV, founded by Mihsha and Jenny — professional groomers with a combined 32 years of experience. We are dedicated to providing top-quality grooming services to keep your furry friend looking and feeling their best, and our team is committed to ensuring a comfortable, enjoyable experience for every dog that walks through our doors.

Our Passion For Pups

At Pawfect Beauty, our love for dogs is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to create a nurturing and soothing environment where your pet can relax and enjoy the grooming process.

Tailored Grooming Services

From bathing and brushing to breed-specific trims, we offer a wide range of grooming services to cater to your furbaby's unique needs. Our personalized approach ensures that your pet receives the individual care and attention they deserve.

Convenient Appointment Hours

We understand the busy schedules of pet parents, which is why we offer flexible appointment hours to accommodate your convenience. Contact us to book an appointment and treat your dog to a pampering session at Pawfect Beauty.
