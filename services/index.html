Our Services

Pawfect Beauty is a women-owned dog grooming salon in Las Vegas, NV. Mihsha and Jenny bring a combined 32 years of experience to every groom — and every dog is treated like family.

Full Groom

Our Full Groom includes a soothing bath, a custom haircut (breed or your choice), ear cleaning, nail trim and dremmel, tidy paws and sanitary, plus anal glands expression. All the essentials, all the glam!

Ideal for Doodles, Poodles, Schnauzers, Shih Tzus, Bichons, and any long-haired or double-coated breed that needs regular shaping. Most appointments take 2–3 hours. We recommend scheduling every 6–8 weeks to keep coats from matting.

Bath & Tidy

Our Bath & Tidy pampers your pup with a luxurious bath, thorough brush-out, ear cleaning, nail trim and dremmel, and anal gland expression. No full haircut—just a neat touch-up on the face, butt feathers, and all those little spots that keep them dashing!

A great fit for short-coated breeds like Labs, Beagles, and Boxers, or long-haired dogs between full grooms who just need a freshen-up. Also a perfect first appointment for puppies. Most appointments take 1.5–2 hours.

Bath & Nails

For the Bath & Nails, think of it as a streamlined sparkle: a refreshing bath, gentle brushing, ear cleaning, nail trim and file, plus anal gland expression. No haircut—just a sleek, clean pup, ready to strut their natural style!

The go-to option for low-maintenance coats or pups who just need a quick refresh between fuller grooms. Appointments typically run 1–1.5 hours.

Express Service — No Kennel Time

Add Express to any service for just $20. Your dog is groomed start to finish without being placed in a kennel — one groomer, no cage time, no waiting around with other dogs.

Especially popular for anxious dogs, senior pups who need extra comfort, and dogs who've had stressful grooming experiences in the past.

Anxious, Nervous & Senior Dogs

Jenny and Mihsha has spent years working with dogs who need a little extra patience — seniors, rescues, and nervous pups who've never had a good experience. If your dog has been turned away elsewhere, we want to meet them.

We work with all temperaments and ages. Just let us know your dog's needs when you book and we'll make sure they're comfortable from the moment they walk in.
